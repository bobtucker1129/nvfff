# NVFFF — Northern Virginia Fly Fishing Fanatics

## Product Purpose
A community platform for fly fishing enthusiasts in Northern Virginia. Members log catches, save private fishing spots ("honey holes"), share public catches on a map, and connect with other anglers.

## Users
- **Core**: Local fly fishers in Northern Virginia, ages 25-60
- **Vibe**: Working-class and professional anglers who value authenticity over polish. They drink craft beer, drive trucks with rod racks, and know the difference between a sulphur and a BWO hatch.
- **Tech comfort**: Moderate. They use smartphones for everything but don't want complexity.
- **Emotional need**: Belonging to a crew that gets it. Not a corporate outdoor brand. Not Instagram perfection. Real fishing, real people.

## Brand
- **Name**: NVFFF (Northern Virginia Fly Fishing Fanatics)
- **Tone**: Unpretentious, warm, slightly irreverent. Like your buddy who always knows where the fish are biting.
- **Anti-references**: No REI catalog aesthetic. No "live laugh love" outdoorsy vibes. No SaaS dashboard coldness.
- **References**: Old Orvis catalogs, local dive bars, weathered wooden boat docks, hand-tied flies, topo maps with coffee stains.

## Strategic Principles
1. Authenticity over polish — a little rough around the edges is fine
2. Community first — the product serves relationships, not data collection
3. Local pride — this is NOVA water, not generic "mid-Atlantic"
4. Respect the resource — conservation without preaching
